Product Information

How should I store my ceremonial grade matcha?

add
To preserve the vibrant color and delicate flavor profile of our matcha, store it in its original airtight tin in a cool, dark place. For maximum freshness after opening, we recommend refrigeration. Always allow the tin to reach room temperature before opening to prevent condensation.

Are your teas organic and sustainable?

add
We partner exclusively with small-scale farmers in Uji and Shizuoka who practice traditional, sustainable harvesting methods. While some of our specific cultivars are in transition to formal organic certification, all are grown without synthetic pesticides.

What is the shelf life of loose leaf teas?

add
Our green teas are best enjoyed within 6 months of purchase. Oolongs and Black teas maintain their integrity for up to 18 months if kept in a cool, dry environment away from strong odors.

Shipping & Returns

Do you offer international shipping?

add
Yes, we ship to over 40 countries worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination. Please note that customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient.

What is your return policy for tea products?

add
Due to the perishable nature of our products, we cannot accept returns on tea. However, if your order arrives damaged or incorrect, please contact us within 7 days for a replacement.

Orders

Can I modify my order after it has been placed?

add
We process orders quickly to ensure freshness. If you need to make a change, please email us within 2 hours of placing your order. Once an order has been fulfilled, we are unable to make modifications.

Do you offer wholesale opportunities?

add
We work with a select number of boutique cafes, tea houses, and retailers. Please visit our Wholesale page or contact our partnership team for more information.
