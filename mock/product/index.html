The Art of the First Flush

Our Uji Reserve is meticulously selected from the very first harvest of the spring season. This period, known as 'Shincha', produces leaves that are bursting with nutrients and a natural sweetness that cannot be replicated.

Shaded for 21 days before harvest, the tea plants increase their chlorophyll and L-theanine levels, resulting in that iconic deep green color and the calm, focused energy that premium matcha is famous for.
